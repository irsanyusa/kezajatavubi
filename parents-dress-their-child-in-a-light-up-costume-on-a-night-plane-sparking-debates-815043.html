Viral Video: Childs Glowing Outfit on a Dark Plane Causes Reactions

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 30, 2024

Passengers on a plane are expected to be tolerant and calm towards little kids on board. However, sometimes, things go beyond the borders and cause disturbance to everyone.

A recent video posted on sarcasm_only Instagram page where a girl of about 5 or 6 years old is wearing a light-up costume went extremely viral on the Internet.

The video shows a crowded flight of people sitting in a dark cabin, suggesting it was a night trip, with the lights turned off so that people could sleep. A caption with the footage said, “Imagine being on this flight... I’d be sick.”

The video gathered about 7k comments from people debating whether the parents should have been more careful choosing their kid’s costume or whether it is fine to have such a blinding light show on a red-eye flight.

But most of them were for showing more respect to other passengers:

We should all respect each other and be mindful of the inconveniences we might cause others, especially when we have little kids. We should not take advantage of the situation and demand that other people give up their seats for us, like in this article.
