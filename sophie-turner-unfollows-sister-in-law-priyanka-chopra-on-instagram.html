Sophie Turner has reportedly unfollowed her sister-in-law Priyanka Chopra amid the Game of Thrones actress’ divorce from Joe Jonas. Chopra (who is married to Joe’s brother Nick) has apparently also unfollowed Turner.

Sophie Turner Unfollows Priyanka Chopra on Instagram

According to Page Six, Turner and Chopra no longer follow each other on Instagram. However, Turner reportedly still follows Kevin Jonas’ wife Danielle, as well as her estranged husband Joe. Representatives for Turner and Chopra apparently did not respond to a request for comment.

Chopra has not publicly commented on Turner’s divorce from Joe, although Page Six points out that she posted on Instagram about her “dreamy” husband Nick shortly after news broke of the couple’s split in September.

While rumors were swirling about the couple heading for divorce, Chopra and Nick were showing their love at the Jonas Brothers’ concert in Austin, Texas. Chopra was in the crowd as her husband performed, and he was caught touching her hand at one point.

The Couple Reached a Temporary Custody Agreement

Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas met for a multi-day mediation last week, and the couple agreed to a temporary custody arrangement for their two daughters. The kids will be with Turner until October 21, and she’s allowed to take them to the UK.

The couple will then alternate their time with the kids through the end of the year, with Jonas getting them for Thanksgiving and Turner getting them for Christmas and New Year’s.

Jonas filed for divorce from Turner last month, and the couple released a statement saying they had “mutually decided to amicably end our marriage.”

Page Six reports that the couple will be settling their divorce in private. According to court documents, they have reached “various agreements” and are planning to “pursue an amicable resolution of all issues.”
