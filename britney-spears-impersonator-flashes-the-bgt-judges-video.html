In 2011, one act shocked the judges on BGT with a nearly-naked routine. The contestant’s name was Lorna Bliss, and she was a Britney Spears impersonator.

Lorna showed so much skin during her audition that the show had to censor her breasts for the entire performance. Check out the NSFW routine below, and read on to find out what the judges thought.

‘BGT’ Contestant Lorna Bliss Strips For The Judges

Lorna shared that she had been impersonating Britney since 2000. She said she “tightens up” when Britney does, and eats Pizza Hut when Britney puts on weight. She even shaved her head when Britney shaved hers!

Lorna kicked off her audition by stripping off her cardigan and revealing the skimpy outfit underneath. She wore a nude thong, with the rest of her body covered in rhinestones a la Britney’s “Toxic” music video.

She went on to perform the song while dancing around provocatively. Eventually, she made her way down to the judges, kissing Amanda Holden and even lying down on the table.

When she returned to the stage, the crowd began to boo her. The judges then hit their red buzzers, ending Lorna’s performance. She mistakenly ran off stage, and had to be told to go back out by hosts Ant and Dec.

What Did The Judges Think Of Lorna’s Act?

Judge Michael McIntyre was jealous that Amanda got a kiss and he didn’t. Amanda said she “enjoyed” the performance, telling Lorna she liked the taste of her “cherry lip gloss.”

Lorna received three “no” votes from the judges, but she was brought back for the semifinals. Amanda said David Hasselhoff “put up a very good case for her.” Lorna performed “I’m a Slave 4 U” with a snake over her shoulders, then transitioned into “Oops!…I Did It Again.” All the judges except Amanda buzzed the performance.

In 2012, Lorna tried out for X Factor UK. She performed “Till the World Ends” by Britney Spears in a mesh body stocking, at one point straddling judge Louis Walsh and chasing Gary Barlow around the auditorium.

Tulisa told Lorna she was just in it for the fame. Gary called it “shock value.” Needless to say, they didn’t send Lorna to the next round.
