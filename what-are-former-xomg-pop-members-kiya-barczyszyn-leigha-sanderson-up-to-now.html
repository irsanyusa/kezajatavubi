What Are Former XOMG POP! Members Kiya Barczyszyn, Leigha Sanderson Up To Now?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Kiya Barczyszyn and Leigha Rose Sanderson, once integral members of the popular girl group XOMG POP!, have embarked on unique paths following their departures from the group. As part of the ensemble formed on the reality show Siwas Dance Pop Revolution, their journey from XOMG POP! has taken them in different directions, showcasing their individual talents and aspirations.

What Are Kiya Barczyszyn, Leigha Sanderson Up To Now?

XOMG POP! is recognized for their catchy tunes and dynamic performances, and has captured the hearts of many young fans. However, as is the nature of the entertainment industry, changes occurred when both Barczyszyn and Sanderson announced their exits from the group in September 2022 and May 2023 respectively.

Barczyszyn, who’s known for her distinctive voice and stage presence, embraced a new opportunity by joining the ranks of the Kidz Bop Kids. This esteemed group features talented child singers who create vibrant covers of popular songs. Beyond her involvement with Kidz Bop, Barczyszyn has also ventured into the realm of solo music, demonstrating her versatility as an artist.

On the other hand, Sanderson has taken a distinctive path since her departure from XOMG POP! In contrast to pursuing a solo music career, Sanderson has immersed herself in the world of dance. Recent glimpses into her journey reveal her dedication to refining her dance skills, as she attends dance classes and passionately engages in various dance routines.

Both Barczyszyn and Sanderson, despite parting ways with XOMG POP!, remain steadfast in their pursuit of success. As young and talented individuals, they have endless possibilities filling their futures.
Barczyszyn’s involvement in Kidz Bop and her multi-faceted endeavors, combined with Sanderson’s focus on her music, fashion, and beauty ventures, position these two artists to leave their mark on the entertainment industry.

Allegations of Unprofessionalism Emerge After Barczyszyn, Sanderson Left

As the fallout from the departures of Sanderson and Barczyszyn from the girl group XOMG POP! continues, allegations of unprofessional behavior and secrecy have cast a shadow over the group’s image. This isn’t the first time a member has left XOMG POP!, raising questions about the dynamics within the group and its management.

While no official statements have been issued by XOMG POP! or its representatives regarding the reasons behind these departures, fans and followers continue to speculate on the circumstances surrounding Sanderson and Barczyszyn’s exits.

Some have questioned the lack of transparency and acknowledgment from the group in relation to the departing members, further fueling rumors and discussions about the group’s future. As more information emerges, the public remains eager to understand the full story behind these departures and the potential impact on the group’s reputation moving forward.
