It’s no surprise that Terry Crews, ‘America’s Got Talent‘ host has received the Sexiest Host Alive award! He’s been a host on AGT for 2 seasons now, including ‘Champions’ and let’s be honest, there’s no better compliment than this.

It’s official… Terry crews takes home gold!

Crews is by far one of the best, if not THE BEST host ‘America’s Got Talent’ has ever seen. He’s likeable, he’s energetic and he’s… sexy! People’s Magazine released their list for Sexiest Men Alive and undoubtedly Terry took the TV host slot. It’s not new for the AGT host and actor to receive such awards and has been titled with similar awards in the past.

Do you think Crews deserved the award? Let us know!

Who else made the list?

Other categories included Sexiest Reality Star, Sexiest Superhero and Sexiest Heartthrob. Here are the winners:

Reality Star – Antoni Porowksi

Superhero – Tom Holland

Heartthrob – David Dobrik

Funny Guy – Trevor Noah

Here’s what Terry had to say about the list

Crews said that he ‘would never’ put himself on the list and would pick Dwyane Johnson instead. How modest of you Crews! At least he was honest enough to say that his taste in fashion is impeccable and we’re not in disagreement on that one, that’s for sure!

Terry also spoke about how much he loves doing AGT and that nothing compares to his excitement about doing the show. That’s right, not even Brooklyn 99!

Crews isn’t going anywhere and is BACK for AGT in 2020!

That’s right. Terry Crews is not going anywhere and will continue to host for both the main series and ‘The Champions’ which will air early next year with new judge Alesha Dixon. First up… the suits! We can’t get enough of them and we’re sure the audience can’t either. Throughout the show, the host dazzled in an array of different colours from mint green to beige, the honest truth is, we love them all!
