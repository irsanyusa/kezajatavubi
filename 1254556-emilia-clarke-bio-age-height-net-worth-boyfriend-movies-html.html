Emilia Clarke's family background and early life

The actress and her brother were brought up in Berkshire, England. Her father is a sound engineer in a theatre while her mother works in the marketing department of a business organisation.

The actress loved acting from a tender age after watching Show Boat, a musical in which her dad was working. Her parents encouraged and supported her to become an actress.

The actress previously reported that she has Indian roots. Her grandmother was born from a secret affair that her great-grandmother had with an Indian man.

Her grandmother inherited a darker complexion from her Indian father. She would wear light makeup to hide her skin colour. She admitted that her grandmother's life of trying to appear lighter in complexion must have been difficult.

Emilia Clarke education
