VIDEO: Kourtney Kardashian, 44, Pregnant With Travis Barkers Baby, Shares Behind-the-Scene Moment

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 30, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian is expecting her first child with husband Travis Barker, 47. On Sunday, The Kardashians producer-star delighted fans by sharing additional exclusive photos from her unexpected pregnancy announcement. The reveal took place during the recent Blink-182 concert at the renowned BMO Stadium in Los Angeles, which occurred last Friday.

Sharing a series of heartwarming behind-the-scene photos.

Within the slideshow posted on Instagram, the 44-year-old soon-to-be mother showcased a heartwarming moment where her neighbor-turned-husband, Travis Barker, affectionately kissed her growing baby bump while they were backstage at the concert venue.

Kourtney Kardashian, who relies on the expertise of stylist Chris B. Kim, made a fashion statement during her official pregnancy announcement. Opting for a black mesh bodysuit that revealed her bra, she paired it with black leather pants.

In one of the black and white photos, the founder of Poosh, who has an impressive following of 222 million on social media, was seen using a black marker to create a sign that read: “Travis, I’m Pregnant.”

The “unexpected” pregnancy announcement.

Surrounded by her niece North West, her daughter Penelope Scotland Disick, and stepdaughter Alabama Luella Barker, Kourtney proudly held up her poster, making a playful reference to Blink-182’s music video for All the Small Things from 2010. To the surprise of many, the 47-year-old drummer, Travis, who pretended to be unaware of the pregnancy, left his drum kit and jumped into the VIP area of the orchestra pit to publicly embrace Kourtney.

However, Travis’ second ex-wife, Shanna Moakler, who was crowned Miss USA in 1995, confirmed on Instagram that she had known about the pregnancy for weeks. She was also present in the audience that night to support their 19-year-old son, Landon Asher Barker, who was opening for his famous father. Also present in the cheering audience was Barker’s youngest child, 17-year-old daughter Alabama Luella Barker, born in Rhode Island.

The pair already share kids with previous partners.

In addition to her role as stepmother to Barker’s 24-year-old stepdaughter Atiana De La Hoya from his previous marriage to Shanna, which lasted three years and ended in 2008, the half-Armenian socialite is also a mother to three children from her previous relationship with Scott Disick.

Having been married for 14 months, the couple celebrated their union with three wedding ceremonies in 2022, held on April 4, May 15, and May 22, following a whirlwind 16-month romance.

The Grammy-nominated rock band, Blink-182, is embarking on an extensive year-long tour, with two significant breaks scheduled from July 17 to August 31 and October 23 to February 7.

Meanwhile, Kourtney Kardashian is currently involved in the executive production and starring role for the third season of her family’s popular spin-off series, Keeping Up with the Kardashians. In addition to her television commitments, the star stays occupied by managing her vitamin supplement company, Lemmelive, and overseeing her lifestyle and wellness website, Poosh.

In her caption accompanying the post, Kourtney expressed overwhelming gratitude and joy for the divine blessing and plan bestowed upon her by God. It is worth noting that Kourtney made the decision to discontinue her IVF treatments back in February 2022. Travis has demonstrated genuine love and support for Kourtney throughout the difficult process.
