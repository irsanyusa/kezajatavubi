On Spains Got Talent, a very funny clown surprised the judges with his voice. What started off as a comedic routine with his partner turned into an emotional audition. The judges and audience were left breathless when the clown began to sing opera.

Mindanguillo and Manolo Make Judges Laugh

Manolo Acosta came to ‘Spains Got Talent’ this year with his clown friend Mindanguillo. According to Acosta, Mindanguillo has always wanted to sing in front of a huge audience. The only issue is, he is extremely shy. When Manolo speaks to him, he decides to start throwing confetti on him. Acosta then leaves the stage so the clown can do his performance, but becomes embarrassed and follows him backstage.

When the music begins to play everyone is surprised because they are expecting a comedic routine.

“Is that the music?” Says Santi Millan.

Mindanguillo proceeds to sing Opera music and all the judges, and the audience, are speechless. He continues to do magic routines while performing.

The judges are full of emotion and Mindanguillo gets a standing ovation.

“It was spectacular I could spend hours and hours watching you,” said Risto Mejide. When it was Dani Martinez’s turn he throws confetti at him, and the host tells him jokingly to stop because they have to clean that up. He also presses the reject button on him by mistake making the clown sad.

“For me, it’s a super yes,” said Martinez.

The comedic pair end up getting three yeses and hug.

Where Are They Now?

Prior to their spotlight on Spains Got Talent, the pair have been performing their comedic routines for years. In 2018 they had a show called “Tu Eres La Magia” for which they were nominated for many awards. They also have their own theatre called MyM Teatro where they perform comedic routines and have other comedic groups perform.

The pair continue to travel and perform, and have even been featured as some of the greatest performers in Europe right now.
