Matt James biography

Matt was born on December 5, 1991, in Raleigh, North Carolina. As of 2021, Matt James age is 29 years.

The real estate broker and his brother were raised by a single mother, Patty. Their father wasn't around. The siblings grew up in Raleigh, but James moved to New York City as an adult.

Education and career in football

The North Carolina native attended Wake Forest University (2010 to 2014), where he majored in Economics and played as a wide receiver on the school's football team, the Wake Forest Demon Deacons.

While on the team, he met Bachelorette alum Tyler Cameron, who played quarterback in 2012 and 2013. James was called to play for the NFL's Carolina Panthers and New Orleans Saints but was eventually cut from both teams.

What ethnicity is Matt James?

James is biracial - his mother is White his father is Black. He, however, identifies as a Black man.

In an interview with Variety, Matt revealed:

I identify as Black because I am. If you didn't know me personally, you wouldn't know that my mom is white. I don't have a tag on my shirt or a sign on my head that's saying like, "My mom is white." If you see me, you're like, "Oh, that's a Black dude" [or] "yeah, that tall Black guy in my class." It's [not] like, "That biracial guy." I don't present as that. That doesn't mean that's not who I am, but I'm viewed and my experience has been as a Black man, and that's what I am, regardless of my mom being white.

What does Matt James do for a living?

After his graduation from Wake Forest University as an Economics major, James landed a job as an associate broker at CBRE, a real-estate service and investment firm in Manhattan. He still works at the firm on a full-time basis.

ABC Food Tours & ABC Food Farm

Away from Matt James job, the celebrity is also a community organization founder.

James' love of food and passion for giving back led to his co-creation of a non-profit organization designed to find creative ways to engage kids from underserved communities in New York City, encouraging physical and mental wellness, while also giving them opportunities for new experiences throughout the city.

A year after moving to New York from Raleigh, James alongside Tyler Cameron started ABC Food Tours. ABC Food Tours gives paying tours to tourists, and free tours to underserved elementary school students.
