American Song Contest co-host Snoop Dogg is the latest model for Kim Kardashian’s clothing line SKIMS. He and his family appear in a new campaign for the holidays, modeling pajamas and loungewear.

Snoop Dogg Models with His Family for SKIMS

Kim Kardashian posted a series of photos on her Instagram account showing Snoop’s campaign for the brand. She explained in the caption that Snoop posed with three generations of his family, including his wife Shante Broadus, his kids, and his grandkids.

In one shot, Snoop poses solo in a pair of plaid pajamas accessorized with some bling. In another photo, he and his family wear matching sets. Snoop also poses in a cozy-looking robe and a matching sweatshirt and lounge pants.

Even the smallest members of Snoop’s family get to wear the clothes. In one picture, Snoop smiles as two of his grandkids sit on his lap. The SKIMS Instagram account has even more adorable photos of the family.

Snoop also posted some of the photos (by Donna Trope) on his own account, writing, “Three generations of the Broadus family for the @skims Holiday Campaign this year!”

This Was the Family’s First Time Modeling Together

“I feel so blessed that three generations of my family were able to come together for the SKIMS Holiday campaign!” Snoop said of the photos, according to People. “It’s the first time we’ve been featured in a campaign all together and we all loved the super soft, comfy outfits.”

Snoop added that he and his family members “will definitely be wearing matching SKIMS this holiday season!”

Last year, The Masked Singer winner Teyana Taylor and her husband, Dancing with the Stars winner Iman Shumpert, modeled for the SKIMS holiday campaign with their kids. If you’re interested in rocking the same clothes as Snoop, check out the SKIMS website.
