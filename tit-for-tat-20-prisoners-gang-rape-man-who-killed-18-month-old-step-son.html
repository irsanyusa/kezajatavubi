20 prisoners gang-rape man who killed 18-month-old step son

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

Daryell Dickson Menses Xavier who is currently in jail, was raped by 20 fellow prisoners  not once, but twice, leaving him severely injured, police in Brazil said.

According to dreamindemon.com, Xavier who is a martial arts instructor and jiu-jitsu expert, committed the brutal act on the baby in September and the child was hospitalized in the Anchieta Hospital, Wansbeck, and died two days later.

Doctors had been informed that the victim had been dropped but CCTV cameras from the complex Xavier lived in with the baby's mother, however, triggered after police find that the injuries were not consistent with a fall.
