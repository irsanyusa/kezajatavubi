Little Women, Tootsie, On the Waterfront and More Due on 4K UHD in Columb

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 29, 2024

“Little Women,” “Tootsie” and “On the Waterfront” are coming on 4K Ultra UHD.

On Oct. 1, Sony Pictures Home Entertainment will release “Columbia Classics Collection Volume 5,” the latest in its series of box sets featuring the most acclaimed, beloved or commercially popular films from the studio’s library.

Along with the three films listed above, “Volume 5” will also include “All the King’s Men,” “A Man for All Seasons” and “The Age of Innocence.” Each is accompanied by a curated set of bonus features. In addition to a bonus disc featuring three rare 1920s features, “The Belle of Broadway, “Ladies of Leisure,” “The Desert Bride” and “The Scarlet Lady,” the films will come in a box that contains an 80-page hardbound book exploring the history and impact of all of the titles.
