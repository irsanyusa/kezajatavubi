What happened to Dr Now, the doctor from the show My 600-lb Life?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Dr Now is an Iranian-American surgeon and television personality who gained plenty of public attention for being featured in My 600-lb Life. He worked closely with obese people on the show and transformed their lives. Numerous people expressed concern when rumours circulated online that he left the show.

For a long time, Dr Now has been the resident surgeon in TLC's My 600-lb Life. He has performed various surgical procedures on obese patients exceeding 600 pounds. He performs surgery on patients who have been turned down by other surgeons.

Who is Dr Now?

Dr Now's full name is Younan Nowzaradan. He is a renowned surgeon, television personality, and author best known for his role in TLC's My 600-Lb Life.

How old is Dr Now?

Dr Now's age is 79 years as of 2023. The surgeon was born on 11th October 1944, and his Zodiac sign is Libra.

Where is Dr Now from?

The surgeon and television personality was born in Tehran, Iran. He was raised in Tehran alongside his younger brother. He is now based in Houston, Texas, United States of America. His nationality is Iranian-American, and he is of Assyrian descent.

Educational background

The surgeon attended Iranian schools for his basic education. He then proceeded to the University of Tehran for higher studies and graduated with a degree in medicine in 1970.

He relocated to the United States of America soon after graduating from the University of Tehran. He was part of the Medical Orientation Programme at Saint Louis University in 1971.

The medical doctor completed a Rotating Surgical Internship at St. John Hospital in Detroit, Michigan. Today, he is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons and is certified by the American Board of Surgery.

Career

Dr Younan Nowzaradan has had a successful medical career spanning over five decades. He started his medical career shortly after graduating from the University of Tehran.

He worked in different hospitals in the United States of America, including a four-year surgical residency at St. Thomas Hospital in Nashville, Tennessee.

During his surgical training in 1976 in Nashville, Dr Denton Cooley reached out to Dr Now. Dr Cooley was the first cardiac surgeon to perform an implant of an artificial heart.

He had observed young Dr Younan Nowzaradan performing a heart operation and was convinced there was something special about him. Dr Younan ignored Dr Cooley's invitation and moved to Houston. He did his cardiovascular fellowship at the Texas Heart Institute in Houston.

The surgeon is best known for his role as the resident surgeon in My 600-lb Life. The show first aired in February 2012 and revolved around five morbidly obese patients.

The first episode followed the life of a 650-lb woman on a seven-year weight loss journey. The medic mainly does vascular and bariatric surgery.

In addition to his surgical works, the doctor has published journals and books. His research publications focus primarily on obesity and laparoscopy.

Dr Now's book, Dr Nowzaradan Diet Plan Book For Beginners: 28 Days Meal Plan And 1000 Affordable Recipes for Healthy Living, is arguably his most purchased work because numerous people are interested in weight loss.

The surgeon is currently affiliated with Houston Obesity Surgery in Houston, Texas. He practices medicine at multiple local hospitals. He also has an eponymous website where fans can book his services and read more about the procedures he conducts.

Does Dr Now have a wife?

The My 600-lb Life surgeon is a divorced man who keeps details of his love life away from the media. Delores McRedmond was Dr Now's wife from 1975 to 2002.

The couple got divorced after 27 years of marriage. His ex-wife, Delores, filed for divorce, accusing him of cruelty. The divorce was settled in 2004, and Delores was awarded 70% of the total assets she shared with the doctor.

What happened to Dr. Now's son?

The surgeon and his ex-wife, Delores McRedmond, were blessed with three children in their marriage. The names of the couple's daughters are Jennifer and Jackie.

They have one son. Jonathan Nowzaradan, the surgeon's son, is a director and producer for My 600-lb Life. Jonathan was born in 1978 and is 45 years old as of 2023.

What is Dr Nowzaradan's 1200-calorie-a-day diet?

Dr Now's diet is pretty popular. The 1200-calorie-a-day diet includes low-carbohydrate, low-fat, high-protein, and high-fibre foods. Sugar is not allowed, and the calories are spread out over two or three meals.

The surgeon says the diet should only be followed for a few weeks. The diet facilitates quick weight loss and improves bariatric surgery outcomes. However, it could lead to rebound weight gain and can be dangerous in the long term.
