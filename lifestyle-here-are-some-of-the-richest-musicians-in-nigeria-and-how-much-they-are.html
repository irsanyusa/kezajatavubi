Here are some of the richest musicians in Nigeria and how much they are worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

In a recent Forbes Africa publication, some of the Nigerian musicians that made it to the top include Don Jazzy, Wizkid and Davido. The list was put together using factors such as endorsement value, popularity, show rates, sales, awards, YouTube views, appearance in newspapers, investment, social media presence, influence and others.

Business Insider SSA brings compiles the list of some of the wealthy musicians in the country right now. This is how much they are worth in asset based on their works, endorsements, properties, achievements, investments, and fame. It may rise or fall from time to time.
