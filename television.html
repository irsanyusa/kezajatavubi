History fo the WWE - On This Day

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Events in Film & TV

Rogers and Thesz Champs

1963-01-24 Buddy Rogers and Lou Thesz wrestle in Toronto, Rogers becomes WWF wrestling champion and Thesz becomes NWA champion

  • 1971-01-18 Ivan Koloff beats Bruno Sammartino in New York, to become WWWF champ
  • 1971-02-08 Pedro Morales beats Ivan Koloff in NY, to become WWF wrestling champ
  • 1977-04-30 Wrestler "Superstar" Billy Graham beats Bruno Sammartino in Baltimore, to become WWF champion
  • 1978-02-20 Bob Backland beats "Superstar" Billy Graham in NYC, to become WWF wrestling champion

Hogan's 1st WWF Title

1984-01-23 Hulk Hogan defeats Iron Sheik to win his 1st World Wrestling Federation title at Madison Gardens, New York

  • 1987-01-26 Hart Foundation beat British Bulldogs for WWF tag team title
  • 1987-03-29 WrestleMania III: Pontiac Silverdome, Pontiac, MI (93,173): Hulk Hogan successfully defends WWF Heavyweight title against André the Giant
  • 1987-05-20 Wrestler Jerry Lawler sues WWF & Harley Race for trademark name "king"
  • 1987-07-25 Sherri Martel beats Fabulous Moolah for WWF Woman's Championship Belt
  • 1988-01-24 1st WWF Royal Rumble - Jim Duggan wins
  • 1988-03-27 WrestleMania IV, Historic Atlantic City Convention Hall, NJ: Randy "Macho Man" Savage beats Ted DiBiase for WWF Heavyweight title
  • 1988-10-17 Rockin Robin beats "Sensational" Sherri Martel for WWF woman's title
  • 1989-01-15 Big John Studd wins WWF's 2nd Royal Rumble
  • 1989-02-10 To gain deregulation WWF admits pro wrestling is an exhibition & not a sport, in a NJ court
  • 1989-04-02 WrestleMania V, Historic Atlantic City Convention Hall, NJ: Hulk Hogan beats Randy "Macho Man" Savage for WWF Heavyweight title
  • 1990-04-01 WrestleMania VI, Skydome, Toronto, ON (67,678): Ultimate Warrior beats Hulk Hogan for WWF Heavyweight and Intercontinental titles
  • 1990-08-27 WWF Summer Slam-Ultimate Warrior beats Rick Rude
  • 1991-01-17 Mountie Jacques Rougeau beats Hart for WWF intercontinental title
  • 1991-01-19 Sgt Slaughter defeats Ultimate Warrior for WWF championship belt
  • 1991-03-24 WrestleMania VII in LA Memorial Sports Arena, CA: Hulk Hogan beats Sgt Slaughter for WWF Heavyweight title
  • 1991-11-27 Undertaker beats Hulk Hogan to become new WWF champ
  • 1991-12-03 Hulk Hogan defeats the Undertaker at 'This Tuesday in Texas' at the Freeman Coliseum in San Antonio to become 4-time WWF champion

Piper WWF Intercontinental Champ

1992-01-19 Roddy Piper beats Mountie to become WWF Intercontinental Champ

  • 1992-04-05 WrestleMania VIII, Hoosier Dome, Indianapolis, IN (62,167): Hulk Hogan defeats Sid Justice by DQ; Randy Savage beats Ric Flair for WWF Heavyweight title
  • 1992-10-12 Brett "Hitman" Hart beats Ric Flair for WWF heavyweight title

Hall of Fame

1993-03-22 André the Giant's induction into inaugural WWE Hall of Fame is announced; no ceremony takes place and he is inducted posthumously

  • 1993-04-04 WrestleMania IX, Caesar's Palace, LV, NV: Yokozuna beats Bret Hart for WWF Heavyweight title
  • 1993-06-13 YokoZuna beats Hulk Hogan to become WWF champ
  • 1993-11-18 WWF boss Vince McMahon charged with steroid distribution
  • 1994-03-20 WrestleMania X, Madison Square Garden, NYC: Bret Hart beats Yokozuna to win WWF Championship
  • 1997-03-23 WrestleMania XIII, Rosemont Horizon, IL: Undertaker beats Sycho Sid for WWF Heavyweight title
  • 1998-03-29 WrestleMania XIV, FleetCenter, Boston, MA: 'Stone Cold' Steve Austin's wins his first WWF Heavyweight tile against Shawn Michaels

WrestleMania

1999-03-28 WrestleMania XV, First Union Center, Philadelphia, PA: 'Stone Cold' Steve Austin beats The Rock in no disqualification match for the WWF Heavyweight title

  • 2000-04-02 WrestleMania XVI, Arrowhead Pond of Anaheim, CA: Triple H beats The Rock, Mick Foley and The Big Show in a 4-way elimination match for the WWF Heavyweight tile
  • 2001-04-01 WrestleMania XVII, Astrodome, Houston, Texas: 'Stone Cold' Steve Austin beats The Rock in a no disqualification match for the WWF Championship

WrestleMania

2013-04-07 WrestleMania XXVIII, MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford, NJ (80,676): John Cena defeats The Rock to win his record 11th WWE Championship

  • 2015-03-29 WrestleMania XXXI, Levi's Stadium, Santa Clara, CA (76,976): Seth Rollins beats Brock Lesnar and Roman Reigns in impromptu triple threat match to capture WWE Heavyweight title
  • 2017-04-02 WrestleMania XXXIII, Camping World Stadium, Orlando, FL: Roman Reigns defeats The Undertaker; Brock Lesnar beats Goldberg, first wrestler to win both WWE and Universal Championships

WrestleMania

2018-04-08 WrestleMania XXXIV, Mercedes-Benz Superdome, New Orleans, LA: Ronda Rousey makes WWE debut, teaming with Kurt Angle to beat Triple H and Stephanie McMahon in a mixed tag team match

WrestleMania

2020-04-04 WrestleMania XXXVI, WWE Performance Center, Orlando FL, Part 1: The Undertaker defeats AJ Styles in a Boneyard match; Becky Lynch beats Shayna Baszler to retain Raw Women's C'ship

  • 2020-04-05 WrestleMania XXXVI, WWE Performance Center, Orlando FL, Part 2: Drew McIntyre defeats Brock Lesnar to win the WWE C'ship; Charlotte Flair beats Rhea Ripley for NXT Women's title
