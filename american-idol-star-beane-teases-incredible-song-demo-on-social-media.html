Season 19 American Idol alum Beane just teased a new demo on his Instagram Story on Wednesday. We only have one problem with this: the teaser was not long enough! He has some big projects up his sleeve after making it to the Top 12 on the show this year.

‘American Idol’ Season 19 Contestant Beane Teases New Song Demo

The clip of the song that Beane posted is very whimsical. It sounds like something Billie Eilish or Taylor Swift would sing. His vocals remains steady and are full of heart. He put up a poll to ask his fans if they want to hear the full demo. Of course 95% of them voted “yes.” This is totally a love song with its sentimental lyrics.

Could this be the next American Idol ballad to hit the radio? The 23-year-old Pennsylvania native wrote that he is “so excited to be writing music for you all” in a second Instagram story. Beane has been keeping himself busy since the American Idol finale which aired on May 23. He took a trip to Nashville on July 4 weekend to visit his American Idol buddies and watch them perform.

As we wait for him to post more original music, his recent cover of “Save Your Tears” is going to be on repeat. He plays the guitar and puts his own spin on the hit song by The Weeknd. Beane also urged his followers to stay up to date with his music releases over on his TikTok account.

He last posted a TikTok video in April sharing a song that he was working on the hook for. The comments were full of support after his shocking American Idol elimination. He got to perform “Searching for a Feeling” by Thirdstory on The Ellen DeGeneres Show that same month.
