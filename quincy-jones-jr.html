Quincy Jones Jr (Composer and Record Producer)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 1, 2024

Profession: Composer and Record Producer

Biography: "Q" is a prolific composer and record producer, working in both the jazz and pop genres. He has produced albums for legendary artists such as Frank Sinatra, Michael Jackson, Sarah Vaughan Lesley Gore, Count Basie, Ray Charles, Dinah Washington, Billy Eckstine, Aretha Franklin, Dizzy Gillespie, and the Brothers Johnson. He arranged and produced the all-star charity single "We Are World".

As a composer, Jones wrote several television and film scores, including "In The Heat of the Night"; "They Call Me Mister Tibbs!"; "Sanford and Son"; "Roots"; and "The Color Purple".

He has won 28 Grammy Awards while being nominated 80 times.

Born: March 14, 1933
